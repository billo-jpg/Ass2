1. COEP

Heritage and Legacy: COEP is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India, established in 1854 during the British Raj. It holds a rich legacy of producing engineers who have made significant contributions to various fields over the years.

Academic Excellence: COEP is known for its strong emphasis on academic rigor and research. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering disciplines. The college consistently ranks among the top engineering colleges in India.

Campus and Facilities: Located in Pune, Maharashtra, COEP has a sprawling campus with modern infrastructure and facilities. It houses well-equipped laboratories, libraries, sports facilities, and student clubs and organizations, fostering a vibrant academic and extracurricular environment.
