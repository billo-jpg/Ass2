1.Conrad Pune

Luxurious Accommodations: Conrad Pune is renowned for its luxurious accommodations and modern amenities. Situated in the heart of Pune, it offers spacious rooms and suites with elegant interiors and stunning views of the city skyline or the nearby lush greenery.

Fine Dining Experience: The hotel boasts a range of dining options that cater to various tastes and preferences. From fine dining at its signature restaurants serving international and local cuisines to relaxed café-style eateries, guests can indulge in a culinary journey that reflects Pune’s vibrant food culture.

World-Class Facilities: Conrad Pune is equipped with world-class facilities including a state-of-the-art fitness center, an outdoor swimming pool, and a rejuvenating spa offering a range of treatments. Whether for business or leisure, the hotel provides everything needed for a comfortable and memorable stay in Pune.

2.Mariot hotel pune

Spacious and Elegant Accommodations: Marriott Suites Pune is known for its spacious and elegantly appointed suites, offering guests a luxurious stay in the heart of Pune. Each suite is designed with modern amenities and stylish décor, providing a comfortable retreat after a day of business or exploration in the city

Exceptional Dining Options: The hotel features a variety of dining venues that cater to different tastes and preferences. Guests can savor delicious meals at its fine dining restaurant, which offers a blend of international and local cuisines, or enjoy casual dining at the café or bar, perfect for a relaxed evening with friends or colleagues.

Comprehensive Facilities: Marriott Suites Pune offers a range of facilities designed to enhance guests' stay. These include a well-equipped fitness center for those looking to stay active, a refreshing outdoor swimming pool, and rejuvenating spa services. Additionally, the hotel provides convenient amenities such as business services, meeting rooms, and high-speed internet access, making it ideal for both leisure and business travelers.
